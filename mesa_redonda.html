Caballeros de la Mesa Redonda

Volver al Inicio

Sir Lancelot

Son of a king who died in war. He was raised by a magical lady. He is the bravest knight, but harbors a secret love for Queen Guinevere.

Sir Bamain

King Arthur's nephew. He grew up in the castle and wanted to be a knight since childhood. He represents honor and loyalty.

Sir Galahad

Lancelot's son. He grew up in a monastery and was raised with faith. He is considered the purest knight.

Sir Percival

Son of peasants. He left his village seeking a better life and was ultimately chosen for his pure heart.

Sir Bors

A noble knight, raised in a warrior family. Famous for never breaking his word.

Sir Kay Arthur's

Foster brother. He grew up alongside Arthur and was always his protector.

Sir Gareth

Gawain's brother. At first, he worked as a servant to prove his humility.

Sir Gaheris and Sir Agravain

Gawain's brother and loyal squire. Though he lived in his brothers' shadow, he was brave and calm, until a family tragedy marked his fate.

Sir Bedivere

One of Arthur's first knights. He helped him since before he was king.

Sir Tristan

Prince from distant lands. He was trained as a warrior since childhood and came to Camelot seeking peace.

Sir Palamedes

A foreign knight. He traveled very far to join Camelot seeking justice.

Sir Lamorak

Son of a great warrior. He sought revenge for his father's death.
