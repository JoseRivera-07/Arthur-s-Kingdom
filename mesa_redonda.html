Knights of the Round Table

Return

Sir Lancelot

Son of a king who died in war. He was raised by a magical lady. He is the bravest knight, but harbors a secret love for Queen Guinevere.

Sir Bamain

King Arthur's nephew. He grew up in the castle and wanted to be a knight since childhood. He represents honor and loyalty.

Sir Galahad

Lancelot's son. He grew up in a monastery and was raised with strong faith. He is considered the purest knight.

Sir Percival

Son of peasants. He left his village searching for a better life and was chosen for his pure heart.

Sir Bors

A noble knight raised in a warrior family. Known for never breaking his word.

Sir Kay Arthur's

Arthur’s foster brother. He grew up beside him and was always his protector.

Sir Gareth

Gawain’s brother. At first, he worked as a servant to prove his humility.

Sir Gaheris and Sir Agravain

Gawain’s brother and loyal squire. Living in his brothers’ shadow, he remained brave and calm, until a family tragedy marked his destiny.

Gawain’s strict brother, known for his arrogance. He conspired to reveal Lancelot and Guinevere’s secret, triggering Camelot’s downfall.

Sir Bedivere

One of Arthur’s first knights. He helped him even before he became king.

Sir Tristan

A prince from distant lands. Trained as a warrior from childhood, he arrived at Camelot seeking peace.

Sir Palamedes

A foreign knight who traveled long distances to join Camelot and fight for justice.

Sir Lamorak

Son of a great warrior. He sought revenge after his father’s death.
