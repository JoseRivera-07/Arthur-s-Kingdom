The round table

In Camelot, where the walls shine like gold at dawn, thirteen knights sat as equals before the Round Table...
"Thirteen oaths under a single flame…”

Among all the places, one always remained empty: the Perilous Seat...
“The chosen one rose, bathed in light…”

The end did not come with enemy swords, but with a secret born in Lancelot's heart...
“It was not the hand of the stranger…”

Civil war devoured the kingdom. Gareth fell innocent...
“Two swords clashed, father and shade…”

Bedivere, the last faithful, returned Excalibur to the lake...
“It was not iron that broke Camelot…”

Valentina Gil Duqe
