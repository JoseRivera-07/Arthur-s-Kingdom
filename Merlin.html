As Told by Merlin

✦ ᚠ ᛃ ᛇ ᛏ ✦ ᚨ ᛉ ᛝ ᛟ ✦

I am Merlin, born of prophecy and shaped by the ancient forces that whisper through the world. Long before Arthur took hold of the sword that would define his destiny, I had already seen his future written in the strands of time.

I watched him grow in secret, unaware of the royal blood within him. When the moment arrived, I guided him to the stone where the sacred sword awaited. With a humble heart and a pure spirit, he drew the blade effortlessly, fulfilling the destiny only he could claim.

From that day onward, I served as his mentor and advisor. I taught him to listen beyond words, to lead with compassion, and to trust those who would later stand with him at the Round Table.

Arthur became a beacon of hope, a king whose greatness came not from power, but from honor. And as long as people believe in courage, justice, and unity, the legend of King Arthur will never fade.

So I tell this tale, as Merlin the Wise, keeper of magic and witness to the rise of a true king.
